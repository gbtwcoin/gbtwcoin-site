How to Buy (Pump.fun)

  1. Click Buy on Pump.fun above to open the token page.
  2. Connect your wallet (Phantom for Solana; MetaMask if EVM is supported).
  3. Enter the amount and confirm. Start small to test, then scale.
  4. If a buy fails, try a small slippage (e.g., 1–3%).
  5. After graduation to a DEX pair, you can also trade via the Live Chart link.
