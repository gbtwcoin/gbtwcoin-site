About GBTW

This project is inspired by the CN show, Regular Show. It’s something I always loved watching growing up—same for many others. Because it was sadly discontinued, my plan is to introduce that energy into the world of crypto! With profits gained from this token, this project is willing to give back to communities, charities, and small streamers who play video games! Everything will be transparent—follow our socials and stay tuned!

How to Buy (Pump.fun)

  1. Click Buy on Pump.fun (button above) to open the token page.
  2. Connect your wallet (Phantom for Solana, MetaMask for EVM if supported).
  3. Enter the amount and confirm the transaction. Start small to test, then scale.
  4. If a buy fails, try a small slippage (e.g., 1–3%).
  5. After graduation to a DEX pair, you can also trade via the Live Chart link.
